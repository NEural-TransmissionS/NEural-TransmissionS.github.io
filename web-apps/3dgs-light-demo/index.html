Why this matters for AI 3D modeling

3D Gaussian Splatting uses many soft, colored blobs to generate 2D images of 3D objects. A camera view is made by looking through those blobs and blending what they contribute.

This demo turns that idea into a small color challenge: adjust the blobs until one pixel matches the target. It is a simplified picture of how controlling the blobs, and how light flows through them, changes the image that comes out.
