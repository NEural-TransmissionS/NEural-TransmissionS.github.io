Join the Lab

NETS welcomes students and collaborators interested in machine learning, computer vision, autonomy, applied mathematics, and scientific AI. We are especially interested in people who enjoy both technical depth and practical impact.

Who Should Reach Out

  • Prospective PhD students interested in theory, perception, autonomy, or ML for science and engineering
  • Master's and undergraduate students looking for sustained research involvement
  • Collaborators from academia, industry, government, and defense labs

What We Value

  • Curiosity about how models work internally, not just how they score
  • Comfort moving between mathematics, experiments, and systems constraints
  • Reliability, teamwork, and thoughtful communication

How to Inquire

Please send a short note describing your background, research interests, and why you are interested in NETS. Include a CV and links to any relevant papers, projects, repositories, or portfolios if available.
